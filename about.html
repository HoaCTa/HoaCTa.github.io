About

Hoa Ta is a third-year student in Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine (UCI). She moved to the United States in June 2017.


In 2018, she moved to Southern California to study at Orange Coast College, where she learn most of her fundamental knowledge about the computer science field before she transferred to the University of California, Irvine (UCI) in the Fall of 2022. Her abstract paper about Exploring Physics-Informed Neural Networks in Partial Differential Equations got accepted to the AAAI conference, where she will present her study in February 2023.


She is learning more about machine learning, computer vision as well as Web Development at UCI in the hope become skillful in those areas. She believes in a growth mindset and always embraces the learning process. She is looking for an internship in the summer of 2023 to learn from practical experience.
